Sean M. Sharp

Principal Attorney

Sean is the founder of The Sharp Law Firm. He is a graduate of the University of Chicago Law School and the University of California, Irvine. In law school Sean worked in the Civil Actions Bureau at the Cook County State's Attorney's Office. He represented the County in civil actions and administrative proceedings after obtaining a Rule 711 license. At the University of Chicago Law School Sean was a federally-appointed Child Advocate at the Young Center for Immigrant Children's Right. He represented unaccompanied immigrant children who came to the United States without a parent or legal guardian, advocating for their best interests after they were caught up in the immigration system. He founded The Sharp Law Firm to provide his staunch advocacy and unparalleled legal services to every client, regardless of their circumstances.
